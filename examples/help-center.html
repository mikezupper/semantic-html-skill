Frequently asked questions

The three entries share name="faq", making them a mutually exclusive accordion — opening one closes the others, courtesy of the browser:

Do I need an account to read the guides?

No. Every guide is public. An account only stores your reading position across devices.

Can I download guides for offline use?

Yes — every guide is a single self-contained HTML file. Save the page; there are no stylesheets or scripts to lose. See the note on .

How do I report an error in a guide?

Use the report link in the guide's footer, or email support@html-tags.com. Include the section heading so we can find the passage.
